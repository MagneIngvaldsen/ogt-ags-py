Unofficial AGS Guide
===========================================


- *AGS* is the **Association of Geotechnical and Geoenvironmental Specialists** (`ags.org.uk <http://ags.org.uk>`_)
- The **AGS Data Format** is the data interchange specification published and maintained by the 
  AGS and published at `agsdataformat.com <http://www.agsdataformat.com>`_
  


.. warning::
    
    **IMPORTANT**
    
    - This information is derived from the AGS official website at `agsdataformat.com <http://www.agsdataformat.com>`_.
    - Do not rely on this data, please visit the official sources.
    - Extracts on this site are for a quick guide developers (ie interlinked html, no pdf).
